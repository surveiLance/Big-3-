# Big 3. One Era.

A visual dashboard celebrating the legacy of tennis's greatest trio — Rafael Nadal, Novak Djokovic, and Roger Federer.

🎾 **Live Site:** [big-3-nu.vercel.app](https://big-3-nu.vercel.app)

---

## Pages

- **Home** — Hero section, stats comparison table, surface dominance cards
- **Players** — Individual profiles with career stats and Grand Slam breakdown
- **H2H** — Head-to-head records and match history across all three rivalries
- **Timeline** — Year-by-year career events from 2003 to 2024, filterable by player

## Tech Stack

- [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/)
- [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/)
- Deployed on [Vercel](https://vercel.com/)

## Why I Built This

I'm a massive Big 3 fan and wanted to build something that goes beyond raw stats — a site that captures the storytelling and legacy behind the numbers. This is part of my portfolio of sports visualization projects.
